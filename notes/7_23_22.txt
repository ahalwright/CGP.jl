MI Hypothesis:  When the number of gates in a circuit is close to the K complexity of the circuit, all gates will by interdependent,
  the mutual information between subsets of gates will be high, and T complexity of circuit will be high.  When the number of 
  gates in a circuit is substantially below the K complexity,  gates will not be very interdependent, and the complexity of the 
  circuit will be relatively low.
Added 9/4/22.  Results show that K and T complexity is reduced with 3x1 8gts 4lb by reduncing from 5 gate types to 4 gate types.
See notes/9_4_22.txt.
